SCHEME NAME
Indiramma Atmiya Bharosa Scheme
Also called: Tenant Farmer Support Scheme, Indiramma Atmiya Bharosa

TYPE
State Government — Telangana
Launched on 26 January 2025 by CM A. Revanth Reddy

WHAT IT IS
A financial support scheme specifically for tenant farmers — those who lease or rent land from others for farming. These farmers were excluded from Rythu Bharosa (which is only for land owners). This scheme gives them Rs.12,000 per year to invest in seeds and farming inputs.

BENEFIT AMOUNT
- Rs.12,000 per year per tenant farmer
- Paid via Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to farmer's bank account

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
- Tenant farmers who lease or rent agricultural land from others and farm it
- Must be a permanent resident of Telangana
- Age between 18 and 59 years
- Must NOT be receiving benefits from Rythu Bharosa (no double benefits)

WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE
- Land-owning farmers — they are covered under Rythu Bharosa
- Farmers not residing in Telangana
- Farmers already receiving Rythu Bharosa payments

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar Card
- Lease agreement or tenancy documents proving the farmer leases the land
- Bank account passbook (account in farmer's own name)
- Residence proof
- Mobile number

HOW TO APPLY
Step 1 — Visit your nearest Gram Panchayat or Praja Palana Centre / Common Service Centre (CSC)
Step 2 — Collect the Indiramma Atmiya Bharosa application form (free)
Step 3 — Fill in personal details and tenancy details
Step 4 — Attach lease/tenancy documents and Aadhaar copy
Step 5 — Submit at the Gram Panchayat
Step 6 — After government verification, payment is sent via DBT to your bank account
