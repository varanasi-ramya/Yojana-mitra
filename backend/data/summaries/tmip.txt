SCHEME NAME
TGMIP — Telangana Micro Irrigation Project
Also called: Telangana Government Micro Irrigation Programme, Drip Irrigation Scheme, Sprinkler Irrigation Scheme
Implemented under RKVY (Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana) — Per Drop More Crop component from 2023-24 onwards

TYPE
State Government — Telangana Horticulture Department
Centrally assisted scheme (RKVY funding)

WHAT IT IS
A subsidy scheme to install drip irrigation and sprinkler irrigation systems on farms. The government pays most of the cost — farmers pay only a small share. Helps farmers save water, reduce electricity bills, and increase crop yield.

BENEFIT / SUBSIDY
- SC and ST farmers: 100% subsidy (no cost to farmer)
- All other farmers: 50% to 90% subsidy depending on category
- Eligible area: up to 12.5 acres or actual land held, whichever is less
- Can apply once every 7 years
- 28 companies empanelled — farmer can freely choose any one
- Government pays subsidy directly to the MI company — farmer pays only their share to the company

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
- All farmers in Telangana — all categories, all districts
- No restriction on economic status or crop type
- Horticulture, vegetable, field crop, and plantation farmers
- SC/ST farmers get 100% subsidy

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar Card (with biometric authentication at application)
- Pattadar Passbook / land ownership document
- Bank account details
- Caste Certificate — for SC/ST farmers claiming 100% subsidy
- Quotation from one of the 28 empanelled MI companies (farmer chooses the company)

HOW TO APPLY
Online:
Step 1 — Visit horticulturedept.telangana.gov.in
Step 2 — Register with Aadhaar card and biometric authentication
Step 3 — Select type of system: drip irrigation or sprinkler irrigation
Step 4 — Select one of the 28 empanelled MI companies
Step 5 — Submit application
Step 6 — You will receive 5 SMS updates tracking your application status

Offline:
Step 1 — Visit any MeeSeva centre with Aadhaar card and Pattadar Passbook
Step 2 — MeeSeva operator submits the application on your behalf

After approval:
- The MI company you selected installs the system on your farm
- Government pays the subsidy amount directly to the MI company
- You pay only your share (0% for SC/ST, 10-50% for others) to the company
