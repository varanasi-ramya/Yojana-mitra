SCHEME NAME
PM-KISAN — Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi
Also called: PM Kisan, Kisan Samman Nidhi, PM Kisan Yojana

TYPE
Central Government — Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India

WHAT IT IS
A central government scheme that transfers Rs.6,000 per year directly to all land-owning farmer families in three equal instalments. No application needed once registered — money comes automatically every 4 months. Fully funded by the Central Government.

BENEFIT AMOUNT
- Rs.6,000 per year per farmer family
- Paid in 3 instalments of Rs.2,000 each
- Instalment 1: April to July
- Instalment 2: August to November
- Instalment 3: December to March
- Payment mode: Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account
- 17th instalment was released in October 2024

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
- All farmer families in India who own cultivable agricultural land
- Land must be registered in the farmer's name in land records
- Both small and large landholders are eligible
- Farmer family means husband, wife, and minor children together

WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE
- Institutional landholders such as trusts and companies
- Farmer families where any member is a current or former central or state government employee
- Current or former Members of Parliament, MLAs, Ministers
- Professionals such as doctors, engineers, lawyers, chartered accountants who are also farmers
- Income tax payers
- Pensioners receiving Rs.10,000 or more per month (excluding multi-tasking staff, class IV, and group D employees)

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar Card — mandatory, e-KYC is compulsory or payments are blocked
- Land ownership documents / Pattadar Passbook
- Bank account passbook — account must be Aadhaar-linked
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar

HOW TO APPLY — NEW REGISTRATION
Online:
Step 1 — Visit pmkisan.gov.in
Step 2 — Click Farmer Corner — click New Farmer Registration
Step 3 — Enter Aadhaar number, mobile number, and state
Step 4 — Fill in land details — survey number, khata number, area in acres
Step 5 — Upload land ownership documents
Step 6 — Submit — you will receive a reference number
Step 7 — Application is verified by the state government
Step 8 — Once approved, money starts arriving every 4 months

Offline:
Visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) — they will register for free

MANDATORY e-KYC STEP
e-KYC is compulsory — without it, payments are blocked even if registered
Visit pmkisan.gov.in — Farmer Corner — e-KYC
Enter Aadhaar number and verify with OTP sent to registered mobile number

HOW TO CHECK PAYMENT STATUS
Visit pmkisan.gov.in — Farmer Corner — Beneficiary Status
Enter Aadhaar number OR mobile number OR bank account number
All instalments received will be shown with dates

IF NAME NOT IN BENEFICIARY LIST OR PAYMENT NOT RECEIVED
- Check e-KYC status first at pmkisan.gov.in
- Contact your Village Revenue Officer or local agriculture office with land documents
- Call helpline: 155261
