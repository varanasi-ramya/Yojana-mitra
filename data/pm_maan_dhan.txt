SCHEME NAME
PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana
Also called: PM KMY, Kisan Pension Scheme, Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan

TYPE
Central Government — Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare
Implemented through Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC)

WHAT IT IS
A voluntary pension scheme for small and marginal farmers. If you join between age 18 and 40 and contribute a small fixed amount every month, the government contributes an equal amount every month. After you turn 60, you receive Rs.3,000 every month as pension for the rest of your life.

BENEFIT
- Rs.3,000 per month pension after age 60 — for life
- On death of farmer after age 60: spouse receives Rs.1,500 per month as family pension
- Government matches every rupee the farmer contributes — 50:50 contribution
- If farmer exits the scheme before age 60: gets back own contribution with savings bank interest
- Monthly contribution ranges from Rs.55 to Rs.200 depending on the age at enrolment

MONTHLY CONTRIBUTION TABLE
Age 18 — Farmer pays Rs.55/month — Government pays Rs.55/month
Age 20 — Farmer pays Rs.61/month — Government pays Rs.61/month
Age 25 — Farmer pays Rs.80/month — Government pays Rs.80/month
Age 30 — Farmer pays Rs.110/month — Government pays Rs.110/month
Age 35 — Farmer pays Rs.150/month — Government pays Rs.150/month
Age 40 — Farmer pays Rs.200/month — Government pays Rs.200/month

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
- Small and marginal farmers who own up to 2 hectares (approximately 5 acres) of cultivable land
- Age between 18 and 40 years at the time of enrolment
- Must be a PM-KISAN beneficiary — or enrol in PM-KISAN first before applying

WHO IS NOT ELIGIBLE
- Farmers older than 40 years at the time of application
- Farmers owning more than 2 hectares (5 acres) of land
- Farmers already covered under other statutory social security schemes: EPFO, NPS, ESIC
- Income tax payers
- Government employees

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar Card
- Bank account passbook / savings bank account details
- Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
- Land ownership documents (Pattadar Passbook)

HOW TO APPLY
Easiest method — visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC):
Step 1 — Go to your nearest CSC with Aadhaar card and bank passbook
Step 2 — CSC operator registers you on the PM Maan Dhan portal
Step 3 — Choose your monthly contribution based on your current age
Step 4 — Make the first month's contribution payment at the CSC
Step 5 — From the next month, contribution is automatically deducted from your bank account monthly

Via PM-KISAN instalment (very convenient):
Your monthly contribution can be auto-deducted from your PM-KISAN instalment directly — no need to remember to pay monthly

Online self-enrolment:
Visit maandhan.in
Click Farmer
Click Self Enrolment
Enter your Aadhaar number and OTP
Fill in bank details and land details
Choose monthly contribution amount

IMPORTANT POINTS
- This scheme is voluntary — you choose to join
- The younger you join, the lower your monthly contribution
- Even if land ownership changes later, your pension is not affected once enrolled
- In case of farmer's permanent disability before age 60: spouse can continue contributing and claim pension at 60
- In case of farmer's death before age 60: spouse can either exit and take back contributions with interest, or continue contributing and claim pension at 60
